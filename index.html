The Problem

Many hotels lose revenue because staff forget or intentionally skip entering walk-in bookings into the PMS. This leads to untracked occupancy, inaccurate reports, and lost profits.

Revenue Leakage

Unlogged walk-ins mean lost income and inaccurate financials.

Manual Errors

Relying on staff to log every booking is unreliable and error-prone.

How InnSync Works

InnSync uses smart sensors to detect real-time room occupancy. If a room is occupied but not logged in the PMS, InnSync instantly flags it—ensuring every booking is accounted for.
